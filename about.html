Our Story

Mrs Bake was established in 2023 in Braamfischerville Soweto, Johannesburg. This is a sole owned home bakery company. They provide baked treats for weddings, birthdays, funerals and private intimate celebrations.

Mission

To deliver freshly baked goods, home style treats—scones, jam tarts, melting moments, queen’s cake, and cupcakes—using quality ingredients to bring comfort, joy, and sweetness to every home. The treats will melt in your mouth from being baked with love. The service is of high quality.

Vision

To grow from a humble home bakery into a well-loved local favourite, by bringing affordable, homemade baked goods to families and celebrations. We cater for near and far individuals. We want to bring a taste of joy and happiness with every bite.
